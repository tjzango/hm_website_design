A Creative Journey Begins

Step into the vibrant world of Hannatu Musawa, The Minister of Art, Culture, and Creative Economy. Here, imagination knows no bounds, and creativity reigns as a powerful catalyst for transformation.

About Minister Hannatu Musawa:

Hannatu Musawa is a visionary leader who has dedicated her life to promoting the arts, culture, and the creative economy. With a passion for creativity and a deep understanding of its transformative power, she brings a fresh perspective to Nigeria's cultural landscape.

Culture and creativity unite us, breathing life into our nation. Let's harness our creative potential for a brighter future
Art speaks a universal language, bridging divides and sparking innovation. Nigeria's creative spirit is our greatest asset
In the canvas of culture, every stroke tells a story. Together, we paint a vibrant, prosperous Nigeria.

Vision and Mission

Hannatu Musawa envisions a Nigeria where creativity thrives, cultural diversity is celebrated, and the arts are recognized as a vital engine of economic growth. Her mission is to harness the nation's rich cultural heritage and creativity to drive prosperity, unity, and innovation.
